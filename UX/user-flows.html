App Experience

After transitioning from the landing page, users enter the main app experience where the AI-driven furniture creation process begins. This experience is divided into logical screens that guide the user through the entire process from space definition to final design.

Screen 1: Project Creation & Space Definition

When users click "Try Now" from the landing page, they enter the app and begin the process of defining their space and furniture requirements.

App Screen: Project Creation

The project creation interface allows users to easily define their needs and preferences.

App Screen: Design Visualization

The visualization interface allows users to view and customize their design in 3D.

1
Welcome & Quick Onboarding

The user's first experience in the app includes a personalized welcome and introduction to the platform's capabilities.

  • Brief welcome message
  • Option to take a quick tour or skip to design
2
Space Input Options

Users are offered two main ways to define their space, catering to different preferences and situations.

Option A: Upload Measurements
Suitable for users who already have plans or measurements of their space.
  • Simple form to input room dimensions
  • Visual room shape selector (rectangular, L-shaped, etc.)
  • Quick measurements input with visual guidance
Option B: Scan Space
Ideal for users who prefer a more interactive approach or don't have precise measurements.
  • Camera access request
  • Guidance for capturing the space (walk around slowly)
  • Real-time processing of spatial data
  • Option to manually adjust measurements after scan
3
Customization Elements

Users can customize the AI-generated design to better match their tastes and requirements.

Color/Finish Selector
Interactive selection of colors and finishes for different parts of the furniture, with real-time preview on the 3D model.
Material Options
Ability to choose between different materials such as various types of wood, metals, glass, etc., with information about their properties and price impact.
Hardware Options
Selection of handles, hinges, slides, and other hardware elements, considering both their functionality and aesthetics.
Size Adjustments
Intuitive controls for making small changes to furniture element dimensions, with automatic checks for structural integrity and space fit.

AI Feedback

The chat interface remains available on the visualization screen, allowing users to request changes and receive feedback from the AI based on their preferences.

Change Requests
Users can request changes to the design through natural dialogue, and the AI updates the visualization in real-time.

Example change requests:

  • "Can you make the island larger?"
  • "I would prefer dark wood for the cabinets"

AI Recommendations
The AI not only responds to requests but also offers recommendations based on design best practices and functionality.

Example AI recommendations:

  • "Given your space, I would recommend increasing the distance between the island and cabinets for better traffic flow"
  • "This color combination might enhance the visual sense of space in your room"

Complete User Journey Example

Meet Elena: First-Time User

The following scenario demonstrates how a typical user interacts with the AFUR AI platform from initial discovery to project completion.

1
Discovery
Elena is renovating her kitchen and finds AFUR AI through social media advertising. She navigates to the landing page and is impressed by the promised time savings. She watches the demo video and decides to try the platform.
2
Entry Point
Elena clicks 'Start Designing Now' and enters the app. She quickly goes through the brief introduction.
3
Space Definition
Elena chooses to upload dimensions as her kitchen is already empty. She inputs the dimensions: 4.5m x 3.2m with a window on the north wall. She selects 'L-shaped kitchen' from the layout options. She sets a budget range: $15,000-20,000. She selects 'Modern' as her preferred style.
4
Communicating Requirements
Elena types: 'I need a kitchen with plenty of storage, an island with seating for 3, and a dedicated coffee station.' The AI asks clarifying questions about storage preferences, island features, and dimensions, which Elena answers.
5
Design Generation
Elena sees a 'Designing your kitchen...' animation with progress indicators. Within 30 seconds, the app transitions to the visualization screen.
6
Exploring the Design
Elena is presented with a 3D model of her kitchen design. She rotates the model to see different angles. She explores the storage solutions using the exploded view. She notices the island seating and coffee station area.
7
Customization
Elena decides she wants different cabinet colors. She types: 'Can you show me the cabinets in navy blue with white countertops?' The AI updates the design in real-time. Elena explores material options and selects matte finishes.
8
Technical Verification
Elena checks the specifications and sees that all dimensions are accurate. She reviews the production documentation preview. She sees the estimated production time: 2-3 weeks.
9
Decision and Action
Elena is satisfied with the design and clicks 'Save Design'. She shares the design with her partner via email. Upon receiving approval, she returns and clicks 'Order Now'. She completes the payment and receives an order confirmation.
10
Post-Purchase
Elena receives updates on manufacturing progress. The kitchen is delivered on time, and all parts fit perfectly. Installation goes smoothly thanks to precise specifications. Elena recommends the platform to friends and becomes a returning customer for other rooms.

This simplified experience demonstrates how AFUR AI fulfills its promise to simplify furniture design without sacrificing quality or customization, while making the entire process accessible to non-professionals.
