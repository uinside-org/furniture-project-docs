Key Scenarios

This project represents a comprehensive study of the needs and usage scenarios of an AI system for furniture design by various categories of users. The study includes detailed Customer Journey Maps, comparative analysis of needs, and success metrics for different user scenarios.

The goal of the research is to identify key interaction points, pain points, and opportunities to improve the user experience at all stages of interaction with the product, from awareness to loyalty.
