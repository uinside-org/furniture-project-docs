About the Project

A Customer Journey Map (CJM) is a visual representation of a customer's experience with a product or service. This tool helps organizations better understand their customers' needs, motivations, and pain points at each stage of interaction.

In our project, we present several Customer Journey Maps that explore the implementation of AI solutions from different perspectives:

  • From the perspective of a furniture designer who implements AI tools to optimize their work
  • From the perspective of a real estate developer who uses AI interior visualization as part of the real estate sales process
  • From the perspective of an independent interior designer who uses AI to enhance their competitiveness

These Customer Journey Maps help identify key moments of interaction, emotions, pain points, and opportunities for improvement at each stage of implementing AI solutions in the furniture industry.
