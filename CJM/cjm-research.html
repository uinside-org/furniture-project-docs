User Needs Analysis and CJM

Comparison of target audiences and their needs to optimize AI solution development

About the Research

Research Goals

This research was conducted to gain a deep understanding of the needs of various categories of users of the AI solution for furniture design. The main objectives of the research:

  • Identify key needs and expectations of different user categories
  • Compare priorities and functionality requirements between different personas
  • Create detailed Customer Journey Maps for key personas
  • Determine priority user segments for the first versions of the product
  • Identify key touchpoints, pain points, and opportunities for improvement

Key Findings

Based on the research conducted, the following key conclusions were made:

  • Furniture production designers and developers are the most promising segments for the first product release
  • Design speed and technical documentation accuracy are critical for professional users
  • Project visualization and design variability are more important for end users and independent designers
  • A balance is needed between automating routine tasks and maintaining creative control for designers
  • Integration with existing tools and workflows is a key factor for successful implementation

Target User Prioritization

Based on needs analysis and potential market impact, we have determined the following prioritization of target users:

  1. Furniture Designer — Highest priority due to direct impact on the production process and ability to become a technology opinion leader in the industry.
  2. Developer — High potential for scaling the solution and opportunity for process standardization.
  3. Independent Designer — Important segment for building reputation and expanding product usage.
  4. Affluent Family — Can become an indirect catalyst for feature development through requests to designers.
  5. Young Family — Potential for creating mass-market solutions in the long term.
